Rules

  1. All discs need to be moved to the rod on the right in order to win

  2. Only 1 disc at time can be moved

  3. Bigger discs cannot be moved on smaller ones

Controls

  • Zoom: W/S

  • Horizontal rotation: A/D

  • Select disk from rod: 1/2/3

  • Change rod: Left/Right arrows

  • Land disk: Enter

  • Mouse controls: click to select the disk or make it land (no need to hold it down)
